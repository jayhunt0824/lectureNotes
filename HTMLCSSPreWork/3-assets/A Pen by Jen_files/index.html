Meet my Pet


This is my good boy, Ace. He is a four-year-old White Shepherd Lab mix. His original owner, Corey Ray, got drafted into the MLB and could no longer take care of Ace with all of the traveling, games, and practices. My husband worked with Corey's Godmother and found out Ace needed a good home. We happily took him in and he has been a member of the family ever since.


Ace's favorite things

  • Playing tugs
  • Taking car rides
  • Hedgie, his stuffed hedgehog
  • Gnomy, his giant green Gnome
  • Noming on a good ole antler or raw hide
  • All the cuddles
